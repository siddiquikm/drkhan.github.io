Understanding Weight & Glucose Connection

Why Track Both?

Weight loss improves insulin sensitivity, which leads to better glucose control. Seeing both metrics together shows the cause-and-effect relationship of your lifestyle changes.

Sustainable Rate

A healthy weight loss rate is 0.5-2 lbs per week. Faster loss may indicate muscle loss or unsustainable restriction. Slower is fine if glucose metrics are improving.

Clinical Milestones

Per ADA guidelines: 3% loss begins metabolic benefits, 5% shows meaningful improvement, 7%+ significantly reduces diabetes risk, 10%+ provides major health benefits.

Daily Fluctuations

Weight can fluctuate 2-5 lbs daily due to water, sodium, and digestion. Focus on weekly averages and overall trend rather than day-to-day changes.
